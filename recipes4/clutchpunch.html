Clutch Punch

Best punch you will ever taste

Simple and delicious!

Ingredients

  • sprite or other clear fizzy drink
  • clear liquor (tequila, rum, or both)
  • fruit
  • lime juice or other fruit juice
  • ice
  • simple syrup, bitters, or flavored liqeuor

Steps

  1. grab your favorite cup
  2. fill with ice
  3. add 2 shots of 1 -2 types of liquor
  4. add sprite, flavored carbonated water
  5. add 1 shot bitters, syrups, liqeuor
  6. garnish with fruit and fruit juice
  7. enjoy
