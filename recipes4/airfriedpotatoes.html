Healthy Air Fried Potatoes

Simple healthy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium to large potatoes
  • bottle of spray olive oil
  • salt
  • Kraft Chipolte Aioli

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes and rinse
  2. slice to desired thickness (thicker is better)
  3. spray air frier basket
  4. eavenly spread potatoes in basket
  5. spray with oil and add salt and other seasonings to taste, stir
  6. turn air fryer on air fry and for thicker potatoes start at 350 for 10-15 minutes and then turn up to 390 until desired texture.
  7. for thinner potatoes air fry on 390 for 30 minutes
  8. gently stir every 10 minutes<, add extra olive oil spray and seasonings as needed
  9. serve with chipolte aioli for dipping sauce
