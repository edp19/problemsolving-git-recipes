Easy Corn Bread

Perfect Southern Cornbread

A simple addition to any southern meal. This recipe is for traditional unsweetened cornbread. This recipe is for corn muffins but is easily adapted to cornbread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Martha White or White Lily corn meal mix (buttermilk mix preferred)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup buttermilk (regular milk to substitute)
  • 1/4 cup of oil (canola or vegetabe is best)
  • 1 TBSP butter to top muffins

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. add cornmeal to medium mixing bowl.
  3. incorporate egg and buttermilk
  4. mix in oil
  5. grease muffin tin or spray with cooking spray
  6. evenly fill muffin cups and put in oven. Bake for approimately 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted removes cleanly.
