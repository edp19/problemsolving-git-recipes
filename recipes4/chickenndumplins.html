Chicken N Dumplins'

Diann's Easy Chicken n Dumplins

This easy southern dish is a soup that will warm your body, mind, and soul. A soup with a chicken broth base with onions and salt and pepper. Just add Mary Hill frozen dumplings as per the package directions for the perfect soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lb raw boneless skinless chicken breasts (for extra flavor and fat use other chicken pieces)
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • chicken bouillon
  • 1 package Mary Hill Frozen Dumplings
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

Steps

  1. In a large pot, warm/melt butter or oil
  2. While oil is warming, chop onion to desired size.
  3. Brown onion adding small amounts of chicken broth as needed
  4. Add all of chicken broth and raw chicken to pot. Add seasonings to pot. Cover and boil until chicken is done.
  5. Take dumplings out of freezer
  6. Remove chicken from pot and chop or shred.Turn temperature to high and bring to strong rolling boil.
  7. Follow directions on the dumplings package. to add to soup.
  8. Once added and returned to a boil reduce temperature of dumplings and stir as needed to keep from sticking
  9. Boil until dumplings are tender and season to taste.
